Ethical AI Development and Deployment

Explainability and Interpretability in AI

Ethical development and deployment of AI models require transparency and the ability to explain how AI systems make decisions. This is where explainability and interpretability come into play. By understanding and interpreting the inner workings of AI models, we can ensure that they are fair, unbiased, and accountable.

IBM's Approach

IBM is committed to promoting Ethical AI practices and has developed tools and frameworks to enhance explainability and interpretability. IBM's AI Explainability 360 (AIX360) toolkit is a comprehensive open-source library that provides various algorithms and techniques for explaining AI models across different domains.

Features of AIX360

  • Model-agnostic explanations
  • Post-hoc explanations
  • Local and global explanations
  • Fairness-aware explanations
  • Interactive visualization

Benefits of Explainability and Interpretability

  • Enhances trust in AI systems
  • Facilitates debugging and error detection
  • Enables compliance with regulatory requirements
  • Fosters fairness, transparency, and accountability
